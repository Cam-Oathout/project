FinalProj Dataset Column Definitions

"Reef" contains sample location name the sampling event number and if the sample is on a reef (R) or is a control (C).

"Reef_Name" is the shortened version of the sample location name

"Reef_Material" is the type of material the reef was built out of

"Sampling_Event" shows if the sample was taken during the 1st to the 6th sample occassion

"Reef_or_Control" shows if the sample was collected over an artificial reef or at a control site

"Year" is what year the sample was taken

"Alosa_sp" and beyond columns are scientific names of species present (1) or absent (0) from the sample
